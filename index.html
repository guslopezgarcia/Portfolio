About

I make product knowledge stick

I've spent the last five years turning technical complexity into learning people actually retain — first in customer-facing sales and support, now in instructional design for SaaS platforms.

At PagerDuty, I built AI-assisted content systems that cut content production time from weeks to days, then used that speed to ship a 13-module onboarding program and a 4-course Runbook Automation certification path. The work translated into over 1,000 enterprise engagements and a 42% lift in trial activation.

Before that, I ran sales enablement at Peek, mining Gong call data and Salesforce signals to find where customer-facing teams were actually getting stuck — and building the content to close that gap. My path started in operations at LATAM Airlines, which is where I learned to explain complicated systems to people under pressure, fast.
